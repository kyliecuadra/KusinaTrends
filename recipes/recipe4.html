CHEESY FRIEDCHICKEN WINGS
;
Yield:4 Servings
Prep Time:10 minutes Cooking Time:25 minutes Total Time:35 minutes
Ingredients:
  • 450g chicken wings, slice in half
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cup cornstarch
  • 400ml vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ cup banana ketchup
  • ¼ cup water
  • ¼ cup spring onions, chopped
  • ¼ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
Directions:
  1. Place the chicken wings, ground black pepper, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, egg, and cornstarch in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Mix well and set aside.
  3. In a different bowl, combine soy sauce, banana ketchup, and water; set aside for later use.
  4. Fry the chicken wings until golden and place on a plate or bowl.
  5. In a skillet, pour the soy sauce mixture and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Toss in the fried chicken wings and continue to simmer for another 2 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese and cover the skillet with the lid. Let the cheese melt.
  8. Sprinkle with spring onions and serve immediately.
