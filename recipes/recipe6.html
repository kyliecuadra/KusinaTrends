GRILLED TUNA STEAKIN ADOBO SAUCE
Yield:5 Servings
Prep Time:10 minutes Cooking Time:25 minutes Total Time:35 minutes
Description The Grilled Tuna Steak with Adobo Sauce is a healthier version of @crucrukatchoo's (Tiktok) tuna adobo, which mainly consists of canned tuna. In this version, the alternative to canned tuna is tuna steak which is high in protein. The dish's unique taste comes from the spice mixture known as the adobo sauce. The sauce complements the flavor of the tuna steak
Notes & Tips Tuna is a lean meat. It's relatively high in protein, but low in calories, which means that it keeps you full longer and stops you from eating more. Grilling or broiling a fresh tuna steak is not only one of the healthiest ways to eat tuna, but one of the tastiest.
Ingredients:
  • 4 x 8-ounce tuna steaks
  • 1 tbsp garlic, chopped
  • 1/8 c. dry oregano, crushed
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 large lime, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Directions:
  1. Combine garlic, oregano, chili powder, cumin, oil, vinegar, and lime juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste and mix till blended well into a paste
  2. Rub the Tuna with the paste and set aside.
  3. Place the Tuna on the grid over a warm fire acnd cook for five to six minutes per side or possibly till medium doneness.
  4. Adjust cooking times for the way you like it.
  5. Remove from the grill, and let sit for five minutes prior to service since it will cook a slight bit more after removal from the grill.
