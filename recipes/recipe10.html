HEALTHY SEAFOOD BOIL
Yield:4 Servings
Prep Time:10 minute Cooking Time:30 minute Total Time:40 minute
Ingredients:
  • 2 pcs lime, cut into wedges
  • 1250ml water or beef stock
  • ½ cup old bay seasoning
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 500g sweet potatoes, quartered
  • 2 corn on the cob, quartered
  • 200g tiger shrimp
  • 200g crawfish
  • 200g clams
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 block tofu, slice into 4 portions
Directions:
  1. Place water, old bay seasoning, salt, sweet potatoes, corn, and tofu in a pot.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil and cook until the sweet potatoes are cooked through.
  3. Add the shrimp, crawfish, and clams; continue cooking for another 2 minutes.
  4. Scoop out and place in the serving bowl.
  5. Reduce the braising liquid to half and pour over the shrimp boil.
  6. Drizzle with melted butter and serve with lime wedges.
