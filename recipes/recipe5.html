GARLIC PEPPER BEEFWITH BLANCHED BROCOLLI
;
Yield:2 Servings
Prep Time:10 minutes Cooking Time:10 minutes Total Time:20 minutes
Ingredients:
  • 300g broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp crispy garlic
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 400g beef tenderloin, sliced thinly
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 4-6 pcs calamansi, juiced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cups water or beef stock
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 125g shiitake mushrooms, sliced
Directions:
  1. Blanch the broccoli florets in boiling water for 45 seconds. Remove and set aside.
  2. Marinate the beef tenderloin with oyster sauce and calamansi juice for at least 10 minutes.
  3. Pour vegetable oil into a hot pan over medium heat and sear the marinated beef tenderloin slices for 2 minutes and add the red onion slices
  4. Season with salt and ground black pepper to taste.
  5. Remove from the pan and set it aside.
  6. To make the sauce, use the same pan and add the butter until slightly melted.
  7. Sprinkle the flour and cook the flour until it turns slightly brown but not burnt.
  8. Slowly pour the water or beef stock and keep mixing until no lumps are visible.
  9. Add the mushrooms and the soy sauce and mix well.
  10. Keep mixing until the mixture is slightly thickened.
  11. Season with salt and ground black pepper to taste. Turn off the heat
  12. To serve, divide the cooked beef into two bowls
  13. Pour the gravy into each bowl.
  14. Top each bowl with blanched broccoli and crispy garlic.
  15. Serve with rice.
