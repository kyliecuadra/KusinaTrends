BEEF PARESWITH BOK CHOY AND BROCOLLI
;
Yield:2 Servings
Prep Time:10 minutes Cooking Time:1 hour and 15 minutes Total Time:1 hour and 25 minutes
Description Beef Pares with Bok Choy and Broccoli is a delicious and nutritious dish that combines tender beef, vibrant greens, and a flavorful sauce. The beef is simmered to perfection in a savory broth until it becomes tender and infused with rich flavors. The combination of beef, Bok choy, and broccoli creates a satisfying and well-rounded meal that is both filling and healthy.
Notes & Tips Broccoli and Bok Choy are suitable vegetables to add to counterbalance the oiliness of the beef pares. It also adds nutritional value since both of them contain high dietary fiber content.
Ingredients:
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 400g beef short ribs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1-star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cloves garlic, mashed
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 ½ tbsp soy sauce
  • 1-liter water
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 200g broccoli florets
  • 200g bok choy
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, peeled
  • ¼ cup spring onions, chopped
  • 2 tbsp crispy garlic chips
Directions:
  1. Blanched the broccoli florets and bok choy for 45 seconds in boiling water. Set aside until serving time.
  2. In a pot, add the oil and sear the beef short ribs until browned and a crust is formed.
  3. Add the star anise, cinnamon stick, garlic cloves, red onions, and brown sugar. Saute for about 2 minutes.
  4. Add the oyster sauce, soy sauce, and water.
  5. Bring the mixture to a boil and reduce to a simmer for 1 hour and 10 minutes.
  6. Season the beef pares with salt and ground black pepper to taste.
  7. To serve, divide the beef pares into two bowls.
  8. Add the blanched broccoli florets and bok choy.
  9. Sprinkle with chopped spring onions and crispy garlic chips.
  10. Serve immediately.
