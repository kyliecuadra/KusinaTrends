DALGONA COFFEE
Yield:1 Serving
Prep Time:10 minutes Cooking Time:0 minute Total Time:10 minutes
Description Our healthy version of Dalgona Coffee brings healthy alternative ingredients like oat milk and coconut sugar. Oat milk is a dairy-free alternative that is also high in fiber and vitamins. Coconut sugar is less processed compared to other regular table sugar, and that makes it a healthier option as it carries more vitamins and nutrients.
Ingredients:
  • 1/4 cup ice cubes
  • 1 ½ cups oat milk
  • 1 tbsp coconut sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp instant coffee powder
  • ¼ cup water
Directions:
  1. Combine instant coffee, coconut sugar, and water; whisk using a wire whisk or an electric frother until foam forms.
  2. Place the ice cubes into the serving glass and pour the oat milk.
  3. Top with the foamed coffee
  4. Serve immediately
