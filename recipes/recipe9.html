NO-BAKE SUSHI
Yield:2 Servings
Prep Time:10 minutes Cooking Time:0 minute Total Time:10 minutes
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1 canned tuna in water, drained
  • 2 tbsp banana ketchup
  • 1 tbsp Sriracha
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper, deseeded and minced
  • 1 avocado, pitted and sliced
  • 3-4 Nori sheets
Directions:
  1. Combine cooked rice, chunks of tuna, minced bell pepper, ketchup, Sriracha, and mayonnaise in a bowl.
  2. Season with salt and ground black pepper to taste; mix well.
  3. Place the sushi in a small baking casserole and level out.
  4. Add and arrange the avocado slices.
  5. Serve together with nori sheets.
