AIR-FRIED TOFUIN JAPANESE CURRY SAUCE
Yield:5 Servings
Prep Time:10 minutes Cooking Time:25 minutes Total Time:35 minutes
Description Another cuisine that originated from Japan is Tofu with Japanese curry sauce. Unlike the original recipe, air-frying tofu is much healthier than deep frying, making it more suitable for a healthy diet. The gravylike sauce is rich, creamy, and mildly flavored, making it ideal with air-fried tofu cubes. The meal is frequently served with a side of veggies and steamed rice.
Notes & Tips Curries can provide many health benefits, and they don't have to be full of saturated fat
Ingredients:
  • 5 small block Tofu, Cut into cubes
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 1/4 tsp garlic
  • 1 Apple, grated
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp All purpose flour
  • 1 Carrot, cut into cubes
  • 1 Potato, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 tbsp Japanese Curry
  • 1/2 tbsp Kikoman
  • 1/2 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tsp Tomato Paste
  • 1/4 cup Red Wine
  • 1/2 cup Water
Directions:
  1. Air fry tofu for 15 mins, 250 degrees
  2. Put the saucepan on the stove, stir in olive, garlic, ginger, and grated apple and cook for two minutes
  3. Sift flour and stir
  4. Add Carrots, potatoes and spices, and stir.
  5. Add tomato paste and red wine, and bring to a boil
  6. Stir in honey, kikokam and water, and bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 6 mins.
  7. Transfer it to a plate, then put on top the air fried tofu.
  8. Garnish with scallions
